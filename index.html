Leading Beyond Boundaries

Empowering innovation and technology for a better tomorrow

Davos 2025: |

Against the historic backdrop of the Wisdom House, where centuries of spiritual reflection meet cutting-edge discourse, Leading Beyond Boundaries brings together the world's foremost thinkers, innovators, and decision-makers to chart the course of humanity's technological future.

This exclusive gathering, held alongside the World Economic Forum, creates an intimate space for meaningful dialogue and breakthrough insights on the most pressing challenges of our time.

Key Discussion Themes

  • Law in the Age of AI
    Exploring new frameworks for AI oversight and regulation that balance innovation with social responsibility and ethical considerations.
  • Privacy and the Rise of Agentic AI
    Examining how AI agents and digital twins can enhance individual and community capabilities while preserving privacy and personal autonomy.
  • AI and Personalized Health
    Investigating AI's role in transforming healthcare through personalized medicine and improved diagnostic capabilities.

  • Special Session: Human-AI Symbiosis
    Join us for an engaging discussion of Sandy Pentland's forthcoming book "Human-AI Symbiosis," exploring the evolving relationship between humans and artificial intelligence, and the path toward meaningful collaboration between human and machine intelligence.

Why the Wisdom House?

In choosing this historic church as our venue, we recognize that the greatest technological advances must be guided by timeless human wisdom. The sacred architecture and contemplative atmosphere of the Wisdom House provide an inspiring setting for discussions that will shape our collective future.

Join Us in Shaping Tomorrow

This invitation-only gathering offers:

  • Intimate roundtable discussions with global thought leaders
  • Open forums for dynamic knowledge exchange
  • Networking opportunities with decision-makers and innovators
  • Concrete action plans for implementing positive change

Leading Beyond Boundaries is an independent event held in conjunction with the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting 2025.

Agenda

Private Roundtable Alignment Sessions

Each alignment sessions will last 60 minutes and will be held under Chatham House Rules.

Location: Wisdom House, St. Johann's Church in Davos, Switzerland

Dates & Times: January 21, 22, and 23, from 3:00-4:00 PM.

Participants: Around 15 invited global leaders.

Tentative Topics:

  • Governance in the Age of AI
  • Privacy and the Rise of Agentic AI
  • AI and Personalized Health

Public Panel Discussions

Each panel will invite global experts to explore important topics related to technology and policy. The panels are be preceded by a 60-minute private roundtable alignment session. Following each 45-minute panel discussion there will be an opportunity for Q&A. Light refreshments will be served.

Location: Wisdom House, St. Johann's Church in Davos, Switzerland

Duration: 45 minutes

Dates & Times: January 21, 22, and 23, from 4:00-5:00 PM.

Tentative Topics:

  • Launching the Stanford Digitalist Papers - Insights on AI governance.
  • Shared Wisdom - Agentic twins for individual and community enhancement.
  • Human-AI Symbiosis - Book reading and discussion featuring Sandy Pentland.

Additional Events

Public Dinner: A communal Swiss dinner featuring local foods will be held on Tuesday, January 21 at 9:00 PM, bringing together approximately 50 participants to discuss AI ethics and related themes in an informal setting.
