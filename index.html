Leading Beyond Boundaries

Davos 2025,
Reformierte Kirche St. Johann

Davos 2025: |

Against the historic backdrop of the Wisdom House, where centuries of spiritual reflection meet cutting-edge discourse, Leading Beyond Boundaries brings together the world's foremost thinkers, innovators, and decision-makers to chart the course of humanity's technological future.

This gathering, held alongside the World Economic Forum, creates an intimate space for meaningful dialogue and breakthrough insights on the most pressing challenges of our time.

Public Events

Several events will be open to the public and can be registered through Luma.

Key Discussion Themes

This year's themes are focused on the intersection of technology and human purpose. Each day will have a closed-door roundtable discussion, and a public panel summarizing the key insights. Click the themes to see one-pagers for each topic.

Law in the Age of AI

Exploring new frameworks for AI oversight and regulation that balance innovation with social responsibility and ethical considerations.

Privacy and the Rise of Agentic AI

Examining how AI agents and digital twins can enhance individual and community capabilities while preserving privacy and personal autonomy.

AI and Personalized Health

Investigating AI's role in transforming healthcare through personalized medicine and improved diagnostic capabilities.

Why the Wisdom House?

In choosing this historic church as our venue, we recognize that the greatest technological advances must be guided by timeless human wisdom. The sacred architecture and contemplative atmosphere of the Wisdom House provide an inspiring setting for discussions that will shape our collective future.
